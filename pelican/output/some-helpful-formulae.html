Here are some formulae that you might find useful:

  1. Quadratic formula: $x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$
  2. Pythagorean theorem: $a^2 + b^2 = c^2$
  3. Sum of an arithmetic series: $S = \frac{n}{2}(a + l)$
